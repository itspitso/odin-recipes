Scones

Description

This simple recipes yields a batch of very soft, delicious and decadent scones that can be served on their or with a variety of sides such as butter, whipped cream & strawberries or grated cheese. These are perfect for any occasion and will have people asking for more.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups cake wheat flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 250g butter
  • 400ml buttermilk
  • 2 eggs
  • A handful of blueberries

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 180 degrees.
  2. Sift your flour into a big bowl and then add your sugar.
  3. Take your butter and while still cold, cut into small cubes.
  4. Add your cubes of butter into your dry ingredients, and start rubbing the butter cubes and dry ingredients until you get a fine crumbly texture. Do not overmix.
  5. In a separate bowl, crack your two eggs, add your buttermilk and whisk together until smooth
  6. Add your egg buttermilk mixture into your flour-butter mixture and gently mix together.
  7. Mix until ingredients are well-combined, taking care not to overmix.
  8. Add your blueberries to your dough, gently mixing until they're well-mixed into the dough.
  9. Sprinkle some flour onto a flat surface such as a countertop, then take your dough and lay it on the surface.
  10. Roll your dough out using a rolling pin to your desired thickness.
  11. Using a cake scraper or a knife, cut the dough into the desired shape of your scones.
  12. Lay out your scones on a greased baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes at 180 degress.
  13. Drizzle scones with icing sugar (optional).
  14. Serve warm or wait until chilled.
