Banana Bread

Description

This simple yet delicious banana bread is moist, delicious and filling. Pair it with a cup of coffee and you've got yourself a delicious snack

Ingredients

  • 2 cups self raising flour
  • 1 tspn baking powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 125g butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 overripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 banana sliced lengthwise

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees Celsius and grease loaf tin
  2. Combine flour, baking powder and salt in a bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream butter and brown sugar.
  4. Mix in the eggs and mashed bananas.
  5. Stir the banana mixture into the flour.
  6. Pour batter into greased pan and place banana slice on top.
  7. Bake for 55-60 mins.
  8. Allow to cool, slice and enjoy.
